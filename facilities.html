Facilities

Next has fantastic facilities - in our opinion, the best on campus. We have a fantastic dining hall, a large spacious area on our first floor that's great for study breaks, a courtyard, a music room, exercise and weight rooms, a fully-equipped kitchen for cooking delicious dinners, a game area, a laundry room, and an Athena cluster. And did we mention that we have a ton of movies and equipment that you can borrow?

During the summer of 2011, Next House underwent renovations.

Established in 1982.
Made lively by Nexties throughout the years.
